# Blera Authentic Experiences — Client Proposal

**Immersive Italian Living | Small-Group Experiences in Northern Lazio**

---

## Who This Is For

This proposal targets three client segments that align naturally with what Blera offers:

### 1. Boutique Travel Agencies & Tour Operators
Agencies specializing in experiential, slow-travel, or culinary tourism for travelers aged 25–65. They need unique, hard-to-replicate itineraries that differentiate them from mass-market competitors. Blera provides a turnkey 4-day/3-night package they can white-label or resell at markup.

### 2. Corporate Retreat & Team-Building Clients
Small companies (10–50 employees) or executive teams looking for off-site experiences that go beyond the usual hotel conference room. Olive harvesting and cheesemaking as team activities offer something genuinely memorable. Groups of 6–10 fit executive leadership retreats perfectly.

### 3. Private Groups & Special Occasions
Families celebrating milestones (anniversaries, birthdays, retirements), friend groups, or food/wine enthusiasts organizing a group trip. The all-inclusive pricing at €1,400/person removes planning friction.

## What We Offer

A **4-day, 3-night all-inclusive stay** in Blera, a medieval village in Tuscia, where guests participate in real seasonal activities alongside local artisans and families — not staged demonstrations.

### Seasonal Experiences (selected based on travel dates)

| Season | Anchor Activities |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Jan–Feb** | Artichoke harvest, traditional pig butchering, cured meat production |
| **Mar–Jun** | Wild asparagus foraging, spring walks to Etruscan tombs, Corpus Domini festival |
| **Jul–Aug** | Tomato harvest & passata day, summer sagre (food festivals) |
| **Sep–Oct** | Hazelnut harvest, grape vendemmia, olive harvest begins |
| **Nov–Dec** | Olive pressing, Cantine festival (November), Italian Christmas traditions |

### Year-Round Experiences (always available)

- **Cheesemaking & cured meat tasting** with artisan producer Davide Paolocci
- **Guided walks** to Etruscan rock-cut tombs and the ancient Via Clodia
- **Day excursions** to Lago di Vico, Villa Lante, Palazzo Farnese, Viterbo, or the coast
- **Panonto** — traditional field meal cooked over open fire
- **Meals with local families** and at trusted local restaurants

## Why Blera

- **Not a tour — a life.** Guests join activities that locals do anyway, not performances staged for visitors.
- **Deliberate scarcity.** We host only 15–20 groups per year to protect the village's character and the quality of the experience.
- **Real artisans, real seasons.** Activities shift with the agricultural calendar. No two visits are identical.
- **Tuscia, not Tuscany.** An undiscovered region 80km north of Rome with Etruscan heritage, volcanic lakes, and none of the crowds.
