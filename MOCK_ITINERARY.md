# Rasna Experience — Mock Itinerary
## Group of 6 | October Olive Harvest | 5 Days / 4 Nights

---

**Guests:** 6 adults (mix of couples/friends, English-speaking)  
**Arrival:** Fiumicino Airport (FCO), Rome  
**Departure:** Fiumicino Airport (FCO), Rome  
**Season:** Mid-October (peak olive harvest)  
**Base:** Blera, Lazio — approx. 80 km north of Rome  
**All-inclusive rate:** ~€1,500/person (transport, lodging, all meals, all activities)  
**Total group revenue:** ~€9,000

---

## Day 1 — Arrival & First Impressions

### Morning / Afternoon — Fiumicino → Blera

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 13:00 | Guests land at FCO. Maria Grazia or driver meets them at Arrivals with a hand-written **RASNA** sign. |
| 13:30 | Transfer by private minivan (7-seater) north via SS1 Aurelia — about 1h 20m. Route passes through the Maremma coast and Etruscan heartland. |
| ~14:50 | Enter Blera from the south along the old Via Clodia. First look at the medieval walls and the ravine below town. |

### Afternoon — Settling In

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 15:00 | Check in at **Antonella's B&B** (3 rooms, 2 guests per room). Tour of rooms, terrace, garden. |
| 15:30 | Welcome walk with Maria Grazia — 30 min loop through the historic centre: Piazza Roma, the Romanesque church, the ravine viewpoint over the Biedano valley. No lecture, just a stroll and introductions. |
| 16:30 | **Bar Etruria** — first espresso/aperitivo in the village square. Meet a few familiar faces. |

### Evening — Welcome Dinner

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 19:00 | Welcome dinner hosted at Antonella's or a local family home. |
| Menu | Bruschetta with local olive oil (from last year's harvest), cured meats and pecorino, pasta al pomodoro (tomatoes from the August sauce day), roast pork, seasonal vegetables, local wine, cantucci and vin santo. |
| 21:00 | Early night — tomorrow starts in the grove at 8:00. |

---

## Day 2 — Olive Harvest Day

*The anchor experience. Guests spend a full working day in the grove alongside the family that owns it.*

### Morning — In the Grove

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 07:30 | Breakfast at the B&B — cornetti, yogurt, fruit, coffee. |
| 08:00 | Walk or short drive (5 min) to the olive grove. Meet the landowner/family. Gloves and rakes distributed. |
| 08:15–12:30 | **Hands-on harvest.** Guests lay nets, rake olives from branches by hand, sort and clean. Maria Grazia translates and contextualises — varieties, age of trees, how yield varies year to year, what makes a good oil. Midway break with bread, oil, and water at 10:30. |

### Midday — Mill

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 12:30 | Load the day's olives into crates. Drive to the local **frantoio** (oil press) — in Blera or nearby Vetralla. |
| 13:00 | Watch the pressing process: washing, crushing, centrifuge. Taste fresh-pressed oil directly — intensely green and peppery. Each guest takes home a small bottle of oil from the day's press. |
| 14:00 | Outdoor lunch under the trees or at a long table at the mill — simple and honest: bread, oil, salt, cheese, prosciutto, wine. |

### Afternoon — Free Time

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 15:30 | Return to Blera. Free afternoon. Guests can nap, wander the town independently, browse the small shops, or sit on the terrace. |
| 17:00 | Optional: short guided walk to the **Etruscan necropolis** at the base of the ravine (15 min on foot) — Maria Grazia gives an informal overview of the tomb-cut facades and the Rasna name origin. |

### Evening

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 19:30 | Dinner at **Beccone Albergo** (local restaurant) — hearty Lazio trattoria menu. Acquacotta, ribollita-style soup, grilled meats, house wine. |
| 21:30 | Walk home under the stars. |

---

## Day 3 — Artisans of Blera

*Two visits to local makers — leather in the morning, salumi in the afternoon.*

### Morning — Renzo's Leather Workshop

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 08:00 | Breakfast at B&B. |
| 09:30 | Walk to **Renzo's workshop** in the village. Small space, smell of leather and wax, tools hanging on every wall. |
| 09:45–12:30 | **Hands-on leather session.** Each guest chooses a project (small belt, card wallet, or keyring) and works through it with Renzo guiding — cutting, punching holes, stitching with waxed linen thread, finishing. Renzo speaks in Italian; Maria Grazia translates. The object they make, they keep. |
| 12:30 | Coffee break in the square. |

### Midday — Lunch in Town

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 13:00 | Lunch at a local osteria or Maria Grazia's kitchen — pasta fatta in casa, seasonal greens, cheese, fruit. |

### Afternoon — Emiliano's Affettati

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 15:00 | Visit to **Emiliano's** — tasting and explanation of his cured meats: how the animals are raised, the curing process, the role of salt and time. Guests taste through a full board: lonza, guanciale, coppa, salsiccia stagionata. Local wine to accompany. |
| 16:30 | Each guest takes home a small vacuum-packed selection of affettati. |
| 17:30 | Return to B&B. Free time. |

### Evening

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 19:30 | Relaxed dinner back at Antonella's — lighter evening after the affettati tasting. Soup, bruschetta, affettati board, fruit tart. |

---

## Day 4 — Excursion Day: Tuscia & the Lake

*Step out of Blera for a day of landscapes, gardens, and a medieval city.*

### Morning — Villa Lante & Bagnaia

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 08:00 | Breakfast and early departure by minivan. |
| 09:00 | Arrive **Villa Lante, Bagnaia** — one of the finest Renaissance gardens in Italy. Guided walk through the terraced water gardens, fountains, and cardinal's casino. Not crowded in October. ~1.5 hours. |

### Midday — Viterbo

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 10:45 | Drive 10 min to **Viterbo** — the medieval papal city. |
| 11:00–13:00 | Explore the **Quartiere San Pellegrino** on foot — best-preserved medieval quarter in central Italy. Narrow alleys, external staircases, tower houses, logge. Maria Grazia leads the walk. |
| 13:00 | Lunch at a trattoria in Viterbo's centro storico — pasta all'amatriciana, local porchetta panino option, house wine. |

### Afternoon — Lago di Vico

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 14:30 | Drive south to **Lago di Vico** — a volcanic crater lake inside a nature reserve. Short walk along the lakeside trail through hazel and chestnut forest (October = peak foliage, hazelnuts on the ground). |
| 16:00 | Picnic-style snack at the lake — fruit, nuts, local pastries. |
| 17:00 | Drive back to Blera (~25 min). |

### Evening — Cantine Night (if timing permits)

| Time | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 19:00 | If the trip falls on the second weekend of November (Cantine Festival), guests spend the evening moving between Blera's open wine cellars — eating and drinking inside private cantinas, meeting locals. Otherwise: dinner at Beccone. |
| Menu | Pasta, roast meats, local Tuscia wine (Aleatico, Est!Est!!Est!!!, Cannaiola). |
