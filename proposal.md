# A Special 3-Day Stay in Blera (September)

Dear Friends,

We're so excited to put together something special for your visit to Blera this September — and especially to help celebrate your **30th wedding anniversary**! Here's the program we've planned for you so far:

---

## Day 1 — The Three Villages Walk
- Breakfast at Bar Paradiso
- Full-day guided hike on the **Cammino dei Tre Villaggi** (~20km, 5-8 hours): Villa San Giovanni in Tuscia → Barbarano Romano → Blera, passing the Etruscan acropolis of San Giovenale, the "Devil's Bridge," and Marturanum Regional Park
- Packed lunch along the trail
- Your own **"Etruscan Wanderer" certificate**, stamped at each village
- Sunset aperitivo at Bar Paradiso (Casa Blera terrace)
- Dinner at our home

## Day 2 — A Day in the Province
- Breakfast at Bar Paradiso
- Morning visit to **Tarquinia** (Etruscan necropolis, historic center), lunch there
- Afternoon: a second stop nearby (see options below)
- Aperitivo at Bar Paradiso
- Home-cooked dinner with Cristina

## Day 3 — Artisans & Anniversary Celebration
- Breakfast at Bar Paradiso
- Morning: tasting of traditional cured meats at Emiliano's farm (doubles as lunch)
- Afternoon: hands-on leatherworking with Renzo — make your own bag or belt
- Aperitivo at Bar Paradiso
- Special anniversary dinner (see options below)

---

## Other Options to Consider

This is YOUR trip, so let us know if you'd rather swap anything in or out:

- Lake Vico — scenic walk around the volcanic lake
- Thermal baths — relax and unwind
- Civita di Bagnoregio — the famous "dying town"

**For the anniversary dinner:**
- Intimate dinner with a local family
- Dinner in a wine cellar (cantina), with tastings
- A scenic spot with a sunset toast

**Other things we could add or swap in:**
- A wine cellar tasting
- Olive oil tasting (if available this time of year)

Just let us know what sounds good and we'll build it into the plan. Looking forward to having you here!
